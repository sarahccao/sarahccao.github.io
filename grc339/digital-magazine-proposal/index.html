Introduction

Femme Film

Femme Film is a digital magazine dedicated to capturing and celebrating female narratives in the film industry. We focus on the stories, experiences, and perspectives of women, including screenwriters, actresses, producers, and other industry professionals. Our mission is to share empowering stories of women who are carving out space in the film world and to inspire others to follow in their footsteps.
